# OCR

A OCR app that can recognize texts on image. 

This App is based on <a href="https://github.com/tesseract-ocr/tesseract/blob/master/README.md">Tesseract 4 </a> and its is first of this which is based on Tesseract 4
This app is made possible by a library <a href="https://github.com/adaptech-cz/Tesseract4Android">Tesseract4Android</a>.
After downloading the Training data, app will do everything offline.
